Inside Art

Special project for the 5th Moscow International Biennale for Young Art

Dates

July 7 – 31, 2016

Venue

Tsvetnoy
Tsvetnoy Blvd, 15, Moscow

Role

assistant curator


Inside Art is a project conceived on the 120th anniversary of the presentation of the first X-Ray image by a German physics professor Wilhelm Röntgen.

The Pushkin State Museum of Fine Arts offered 10 young artists working between art, technology and science, an opportunity to go 'deep inside' its own collection of modern and contemporary art through X-Ray images, original pieces of framing and paint.

Artists

Maria Agureeva, Vera Konyashova, Gala Leonova, Viktor Polyakov, Marina Ragozina, Marina Rudenko, Sofa Skidan, Yan Ginzburg, Ilya Fedotov-Fedorov, Andrey Cherkasov
