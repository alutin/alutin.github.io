About

Ohne prickelnd, sanft

Publication

Date

July 27, 2017

Venue

Spike Art Quarterly
Rosa-Luxemburg-Straße, 45, Berlin


Ohne prickelnd, sanft is an independent zine published on the occasion of an exhibition with the same title and is one of the outcomes of the Saas-Fee Summer Institute of Art in Berlin. The publication contains over 20 contributions by artists, curators and writers — the participants of the summer school of 2017.
